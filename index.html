About Akan

The Akan people are a community living primarily in the countries of present day:Ghana and Ivory Coast in West Africa.

Akan Names are derived from Ghanaian culture. In Ghana, children are named based on the day of the week they were born.

Filling the form enables you to find out the day of the week you were born and reveals what your Akan Name is based on your gender and birthday.
